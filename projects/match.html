Charity project:
Match for Startup Refugees

Match is an open source service that allow Startup Refugees, or other organisations and initiatives, to efficiently collect information about the relevant skills and work experience of people. In addition to information collection it consists of intelligent search and filtering logic, and CV generation.

The service has been designed by Futurice and implemented by Futurice and Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences student group. The project is a part of Chilicorn Fund, the Futurice social responsibility initiative.
